FollowPhoto Privacy Policy

General

FollowPhoto takes your privacy very seriously. The app may access your photos or routes only after you imported them. FollowPhoto does not collect or store photos, routes or any information about them. Photos and routes are processed on your computer, and they do not leave it. All imported data will not be available to the app after closing it. Creating a route from photos or combining routes entails sending coordinates to a third-party server called Open Source Routing Machine (OSRM), consider reading their privacy policy. OpenStreetMap is used to display points and routes, whose privacy policy can be read on their website. FollowPhoto only logs anonymized analytics to bring us ability of debuging the app. The bug report only sends device os, app version, user's time zone, app's screen resolution, and some information about app. And no personal data is included.

Changes to This Policy

You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes. By using the FollowPhoto app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
